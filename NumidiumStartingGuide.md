# So, you want to play Numidium, huh?

Welcome!  

We're excited to have you here!  

Numidium is *not* your ElderTeej experience, so here's a quick starting guide! I'll add screenshots as I can (and remove this warning), but for now, here's a quick and dirty guide for you.  

## Making your character  

You'll start off in the starting room from [Alternate Perspective Origins Reborn](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/174595). Standard RaceMenu character creation, or pick a preset. There are no RaceMenu hotkeys set for preset saving or loading since the quicksave keys are disabled by default.  

Once you've created and named your character, give everything a minute to load in. You'll notice that Biggie Traits will pop up- select a couple of these. Also, for now, one of the traits, Paladin, does NOT have a cool thumbnail. It's like this in the mod as it comes, if anyone wants to provide some artwork I can use, please feel free to submit something to me in .dds format and 1024x704 px size, as that's what the rest of them are.  

After selecting your traits, go into your skills menu and select your first three skills to unlock. The list uses [SALSa](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/174469), which will limit your ability to use skills or gain XP in them. No perks in lockpicking? You can't pick locks. No perks in heavy armor? Automatically encumbered if you wear it. These first three perks are your core building blocks to be able to build on, so choose wisely.  

Once your early build is finalized, go ahead and grab stuff from the starting room. I didn't remove anything, but depending on your build, you're just going to be hammering your carry weight for no reason. Only take stuff your build will work with.  

There is a little shrine next to the statue in the starting room, you can pick from one of the 3 Guardian Stone powers if you so choose. Once you're satisfied, talk to the statue in the room to pick your AP start, then walk out the now open Ayleid gate, through the door, and out to your chosen start! Make sure you drop a save when out in the world, then close the game and restart so you can use SSSO3!   

## Important Mods to Know  

### [Stress and Fear](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/116522)  

Combat will stress you out now. I've also added [Light the Way](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/171532), because let's face it- if you're adventuring in the woods, worried something is gonna get you in the dark, you're going to be scared! Try drinking tea, bathing, sleeping, drinking alcohol, eating sweets, anything you'd try in real life to calm your stress levels down. I *have* at least turned the knob down a little on this- you won't shut down or freak out, thanks to the [Hexed Edition](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/154316).  

### [Apothecary](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/52130), [Gourmet](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/96876), [Journeyman](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/92220), [Candlehearth](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/97542), [Starfrost](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/97536), [Arena](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/33487), [Aetherius](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/26686), [Mundus](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/33411), [Pilgrim](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/54099), [Artificer](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/99619)    

SimonRim stuff to flesh out immersion on a few things. Links are here, I won't expound too heavily since SimonRim is so thoroughly documented and discussed already... but potions, food, inns, encounter zones, Survival, religion, race, and standing stones are all SimonRim stuff here. I do *not* use Blade and Blunt, so the stamina penalties from the tie in there are not included here.  

### [Skyrim - Settlement Builder](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/58021) and [Hearth Craft](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/6601)  

Here's a mechanic I'm super excited to nail down- settlement and structure building. There are builder's workbenches in most cities and you can also craft through the [Campfire](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/667) system with Hearth Craft. Additionally, I have included [In-Game Patcher](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/158681) so you can move/disable anything you need to while building your settlement!  

### [Skyrim's Got Talent](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/50357)  

I know ElderTeej has built-in BCE support, but I want being a bard to be open to everyone here. It's a roleplaying list, be a bard if you want!  

### [Skyrim Realistic Conquering](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/26396)  

Take over bandit forts and drive them out!

### [Missives](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/17576)

### [Respawn Souls-like](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/69267)  

Death alternative mod- should be fairly lightweight and has an MCM you can change defeat consquences to!  

### [Become High King of Skyrim TNG](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/142993)

Do what it says- become the king!

### [Quick Light](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/12633) with [Fuelable Lanterns for Quick Light](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/82809)

Need a light and not a magic user? Not a problem... if you have a lantern and oil.

### [Skyrim Save System Overhaul 3](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/122343) with [Utility Plugin](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/133502)  

You know I love this safe saving mod. Utility plugin, so you should be able to save, restart, and enable the mod. 

### [Buyable Skill Books](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/137571) 

One of the only ways to unlock a skill after starting is to have a vanilla skill book in your inventory and take a perk with it there next time you level up. You'll need these!  

### [Andrealletius' Jobs Overhaul](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/109363)

Do jobs instead of combat! Who needs to be the Dragonborn?

### [Ordinator](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/1137)

I know, this isn't meant to be a power fantasy list! But that's why skills are SALSa-locked... and it's also why [NPCs have Ordinator perks](https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/42022) now too. It's only 10% of people, because if everyone could be an OP adventurer, nobody would farm... but 10% of NPCs can be as powerful as you are.
